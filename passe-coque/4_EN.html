The fleet

The Passe‑Coque fleet is mainly made up of sailboats from the 1970s built in composite materials (fiberglass and resin).
The pleasure boating ‚Äúboom‚Äù of these years can also be explained in part by this technical revolution allowing gains in cost and productivity never before achieved.

Many of these units today find themselves abandoned or in disrepair due to the social and economic evolution of the sector.
The construction materials used unfortunately do not allow for quality recovery at the end of their life and generate significant visual and chemical pollution.

Only extending the lifespan of boats can reduce this environmental impact.
At Passe‑Coque, we therefore favor the revitalization of these boats and the sharing of this resource over the construction of new units in these same materials.
