Welcome to our Association!

The Passe-Coque association, born in 2019 in
the Morbihan department, sticks to the simple idea
of saving and refitting abandonned or slated-for-demolition
boats, to then transmit them
to associations, sailing schools or NGOs
where they'll facilitate access to sailing
for a reat number of people, usefully navigating through ecological, social, humanitarian and united projects.
