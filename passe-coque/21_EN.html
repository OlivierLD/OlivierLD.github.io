"The boat is not a goal,
it's a mean..."
The Passe-Coque association, created in 2019 in the Morbihan department, sticks to the simple idea
of saving and refitting abandonned or slated-for-demolition boats, to then transmit them to associations, sailing schools or NGOs
where they'll facilitate access to sailing for a great number of people, usefully navigating through ecological, social, humanitarian and united projects.
