Nav'Solidaire

The association

Nav'Solidaire aims to recycle and redistribute prosthetic equipment that is no longer used in Europe to the most deprived amputees.
There are around 80 million disabled people in the world who do not have hearing aids.
We are setting up a prosthesis collection in France.

Actions

Recognized as a general interest association, Nav Solidaire transports prostheses by sailboat to limit its ecological footprint and to ensure proper reception and redistribution to rehabilitation centers.
Two campaigns towards the African continent have already been carried out and they do not intend to stop there!
