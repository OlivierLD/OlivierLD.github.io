Alain HAHUSSEAU

Alain, a boat'odidact as comfortable in a vineyard as in a shipyard.
A former cellar master, Alain spent a large part of his career in renowned shipyards. He is now the obligatory technical passage at Passe‑Coque and he inspects each boat from the keel to the masthead to organize their rebirth.
