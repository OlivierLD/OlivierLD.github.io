Le Bateau

The Dufour 2800 is a habitable cruising monohull, built by the Dufour Yachts shipyard. This fixed keel sailboat, rigged as a Sloop, designed by naval architect Michel Dufour, is 8.23 m long. Production started in 1977 and ended in 1983. 1300 units of the boat were produced.
