Invest in the eco-village

The entire Passe‑Coque ecosystem is mobilized to carry out its most ambitious project: bringing together in a single place the values, know-how and actions of our association in the service of a blue economy of transition.
Several projects are currently under study in the Morbihan department and we hope to "drop our bags" during the year 2024 or 2025.
This project is part of a cooperative approach to responding to social, societal and economic challenges in our region. We have therefore opted for a legal structure called SCIC: Collective Interest Cooperative Society.
Once the location has been designated, we will launch a fundraising campaign in order to collectively carry out our various actions around reuse in the nautical sector.
Stay tuned to our news to find out the progress of the project!
