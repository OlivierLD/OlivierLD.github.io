Cap Martinique

with Philippe Benoiton

Philippe Benoiton explains his participation in Cap-Martinique:
“A dream is not meant to stay in drawers. My dream is as old as anything and I would like to realize it now: to cross the Atlantic alone by sail, in one go from La Trinité-sur-Mer to Martinique. If I come to want this achievement today, it is because I feel capable of taking part in it.”

Benoiton is not a name unknown to sailors because his brother Patrick worked for the magazines “Bateaux” and “Loisirs Nautiques” and wrote numerous works dedicated to the sea.
