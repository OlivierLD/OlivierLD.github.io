The boat

The Evasion 32 sailboat is a habitable cruising monohull, built by the Bénéteau shipyard in France. This fixed keel sailboat, rigged with a ketch head, designed by naval architect André BÉNÉTEAU, with a maximum draft of 1.40 m. Production started in 1973 and ended in 1981. 286 examples of the boat were produced. Equipped with a diesel engine.
MELKART is a donation to Passe-Coque (apparently the former owner no longer wanted to sail)!

Associative projects

The Melkart project is to form a partnership with an association to help deaf and hard of hearing children sail (share the sea with children, adolescents and adults affected by this pathology).
