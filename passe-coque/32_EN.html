Refit
Refitting, this is Passe-Coque's skill. A team of volunteer experts works on their knowledge transmission and provides all the required tools to refit and renew the botas of the association. Passe-Coque, it's also a premise in Saint-Philibert big enough to manage up to 5 projects in a sheltered place, simultaneously.
Meeting in St Philibert

Passe-Coque is specialized in refitting sailboats of the 70's (fibre glass) to give those units a second life, in an ecological process.
Our shipyard in the zone of Kerran as well as our referents everywhere in France already allowed us to refit more than 25 boats in only a few years (created end of 2019).

We're launching a new activity with the sur-cycling of some units that cannot find their way back to the ocean, but can be transformed into tiny houses.
