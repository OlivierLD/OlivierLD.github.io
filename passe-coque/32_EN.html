Meeting in St Philibert

Passe-Coque is specialized in refitting sailboats of the 70's (fiberglass) to give those units a second life, in an ecological process.
Our shipyard in the zone of Kerran as well as our referents everywhere in France already allowed us to refit more than 25 boats in only four years.

We're launching a new activity with the sur-cycling of some units that cannot find their way back to the ocean, but can be transformed into tiny houses.
