Gabrielle et Guy, Boat Club Development

Gabrielle (pronounced "Gab") and Guy.
The human and environmental dimension is the backbone of a life journey engaged in ecological, social and societal transitions.
A long career of around twenty years as executives in the industry led them to entrepreneurship in 2014, thus allowing them to act in a freer, more committed, more involved way. Today, within a committed Breton territory, they are carrying out a project on a sailing catamaran with a view to inclusion, reconnection to living things, sobriety, observation of the ocean-climate-biodiversity relationship. Within an ecosystem of committed humans, the activities will combine coastal cruises, unusual habitats, unusual restaurants, training.
