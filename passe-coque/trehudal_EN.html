Nicholson 33, La Trinité

The boat

The Nicholson 33 sailboat is a racing-cruising monohull, built by the Nicholson shipyard. This fixed keel sailboat, rigged as a Sloop at the head, designed by naval architect Ron Holland, measures 9.77 m in length. Production started in 1976 and ended in 1979.
LOA 9.77 m
Beam 3.17 m
Disp 4 500 kg
Engine 12 HP
Boat given to Passe‑Coque by Yves Milza (Transquadra 2003, 2004 with Bernard de Ravignan the same year as PJJ)
