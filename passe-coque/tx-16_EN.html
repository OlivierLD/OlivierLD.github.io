Our story

Sailing as an inspiring inclusive experience.
Issé.O - The difference takes off - was born from the meeting between expertise and an ideal.
The observation of the founders of the association is twofold: The social and professional inclusion of people with disabilities has never been so real. And yet, many people remain distant from employment and society in general.
But then, how can we change the outlook, both among able-bodied people and among people with disabilities themselves? How can we shake up representations about their abilities?
For the Issé.O team, passionate about sailing and rich in diverse skills in the fields of health, medico-social and public health, the answer was obvious: “bringing it to life”, experimenting with inclusion.
And for this, what better way than to be a member of a Hindi-able crew on a sailboat tossed by the swell and the breeze?
We are convinced that inclusion should no longer be a subject in 2022 and that it is an opportunity for everyone.

Our objective

Promote the social and professional inclusion of people with disabilities through the practice of sailing.

Our team

A team of united, competent and activist enthusiasts who put their energy at the service of innovative iodized projects with a positive impact.

Our values

Sportiness - Solidarity - fairness - Eco-responsibility

Our project: Sail together

Propose to Norman institutions, associations and businesses a navigation and reflection program organized around inclusion along three axes:
  • Inspire: Bring inclusion to life through a sailing trip and the creation of a disabled - able-bodied crew. Feel the power of a different collective and arouse emotion.
  • Integrate: Value this unique browsing experience and understand the challenges of inclusion.
  • Act: Create together an inclusive action plan for participating organizations and implement it.
Concurrently with this project, numerous awareness-raising actions will be carried out.
