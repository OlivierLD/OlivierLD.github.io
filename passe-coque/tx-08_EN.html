Afri'Can

FÉLICIE was first of all the first offshore school of "passeurs d'écoutes". The admiral sailboat FéLICIE was bequeathed to us in May 2021 and Jean Philippe Baron was the first referent. Félicie sailed offshore to train future sailors who respect the sea to become project leaders. This is how Floriane or Colin and Aurélie were able to set up their own adventure after being on board Félicie.

Fall 2021 :

Félicie was the subject of a major refit project in Saint Malo.
In January 2022, at the hands of the Afri'Can association and its president and new referent Laurent Cauchy Félicie crossed the Atlantic and returned to bring prostheses in partnership with Nav Solidaire.

2022 / 2023

Félicie focused on Afri'can missions and is now based in Dakar.
It will become the property of the Afri'can association in January 2024 but remains monitored by Passe‑Coque to help with the humanitarian missions that Afri'can has set up.
