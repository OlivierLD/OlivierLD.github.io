Jean-François ALLAIS

Jean-François, known as Jeff, is a true jack-of-all-trades when it comes to the sea.
From the fishing spots of his Normandy childhood to Breton surf spots, Jeff is as comfortable on a windsurf as on a kitesurf.
His 10 years spent in a sailboat rental agency allowed him to explore the most beautiful sailing spots in the world.
A skipper who loves the oceans, it is now locally and without a plane that Jeff devotes himself to his passion and shares it with as many people as possible.
