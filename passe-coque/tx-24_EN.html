Refit of the Super-Arlequin "Saudade"

Voile Magazine

The "Super Arlequin" series had its hour of glory in regattas, notably in "La course du Figaro" - or should we say "La course de l'Aurore" - in the hands of famous skippers.
And Passe‑Coque and Voile Magazine wanted to put Saudade back afloat, to show that refit is a sustainable and accessible way to sail.
To discover in each issue of Voile Magazine.
