Epilogue, from Drakes Bay to Oyster Point, in the San Francisco Bay

Friday, May 20th, 2011
Doing nothing, relaxing in Drakes Bay.

Saturday, May 21st, 2011
Wind has been blowing all night, around 5 to 6 Beaufort. We leave at 6 am, to use the tide under the Gate. We're downwind, that's a nice ride.
Duxbury is behind us at 8:40, and we're entering the San Francisco channel at 9:50, that was fast.
We're at Bonita point at 11:05, and under the Gate at 11:30.
The Bay bridge follows, at 12:20, and we're heading to the south bay. The wind picks up, and we dock the boat at the Oyster Point guest dock at 2 pm, it's blowing around 35 knots. An interesting exercise, wind abeam, in the lee of the dock.

That's it, we've closed the loop.

The next day, we went back to our dock, #5 end tie, the crew of the harbor had nicely prepared it for us.
